SAFETY AND PROTECTION EQUIPMENT

Helmet: It is the most important protective equipment. Make sure it complies with international safety regulations, so that it has a structure that protects the entire head, and a transparent mask that protects the eyes from air and dust to help you see clearly, and it must also contain a strap that helps secure it to the head tightly. Gloves: It is recommended to use gloves at all times, and that they be made of leather to protect the hands from excessive friction with the steering wheel.
